## Advanced Agentic Orchestration for the 1-Man Army

Galyarder Framework is the **Intelligence Layer** of the Galyarder Ecosystem. It provides a comprehensive suite of specialized logic engines and elite-level SOPs designed for founders who demand institutional-grade output with zero headcount.

Whether running locally on a Galyarder Agent or as a distributed control plane via Galyarder HQ, the framework provides the deterministic brain required to transform high-level vision into market-ready products.

### The Galyarder Triad

1.  **[Galyarder Agent](https://github.com/galyarderlabs/galyarder-agent)**: The **Entity**. Self-evolving digital personas with recursive memory, stable identity, and universal presence across Telegram, Discord, and custom APIs.
2.  **[Galyarder HQ](https://github.com/galyarderlabs/galyarder-hq)**: The **Control Plane**. A master governance layer for orchestrating swarms, monitoring task queues, and maintaining systemic sovereignty.
3.  **Galyarder Framework**: The **Intelligence**. 40+ specialized agents and 130+ elite-level SOPs that execute complex missions with mathematical precision.

## Mission Execution Lifecycle

Every task within the framework follows a deterministic 5-stage lifecycle:

1.  **Socratic Discovery**: Analyzing business intent and requirements to distill short, digestible specifications.
2.  **Deterministic Blueprinting**: Mission design via formal verification and Vertical Slice (Tracer Bullet) implementation plans.
3.  **Autonomous Implementation**: Clinical execution via strict Test-Driven Development (TDD) and high-fidelity standards.
4.  **Operational Auditing**: Automated live-environment verification (BrowserOS) and professional security hardening.
5.  **Autonomous Distribution**: Programmatic marketing generation (Remotion) and behavioral-targeted market deployment.
